1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device (e.g., computer, smartphone) when you visit our Website. They help us recognize your device, improve functionality, and analyze usage. Other technologies, such as pixel tags or web beacons, serve similar purposes.
2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Website to function, such as maintaining your session or processing bookings. You cannot opt out of these without affecting core functionality.
Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data about how you use our Website (e.g., pages visited, time spent) to improve performance. Example: Google Analytics.
Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (e.g., language, region) to provide a personalized experience.
Advertising Cookies: Track your browsing habits to deliver relevant ads. These may be set by third parties, such as ad networks.

3. How We Use Cookies
We use Cookies to:

Ensure the Website functions properly (e.g., keeping items in your shopping cart).
Analyze Website performance and user behavior to improve our Services.
Personalize content and ads based on your interests.
Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

4. Third-Party Cookies
Some Cookies are set by third-party services, such as:

Google Analytics: For website usage statistics.
Google Ads: For targeted advertising.
Social Media Platforms: For sharing content or tracking interactions.

These third parties may track your activity across other websites. Please review their privacy policies for details.
5. Managing Cookies
You can control Cookies through:

Our Cookie Consent Manager: Available on our Website, allowing you to accept or reject non-essential Cookies.
Browser Settings: Configure your browser to block or delete Cookies. Note that disabling Cookies may affect Website functionality.
Opt-Out Tools: Visit www.networkadvertising.org/choices or www.youronlinechoices.eu (for EU users) to opt out of targeted advertising.
